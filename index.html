Racking Mechanism

Hurricane force winds from the south-southeast pressed against the east (Danneel St) face of the home. The wind flow was caught in the inside corner where the stub-out meets the main house body, creating a vortex pressure zone that amplified forces at the rear-right corner. This concentrated lateral load caused the rear foundation to rack, evidenced by shear cracking along rear seam walls and separation at the rear of the home.
